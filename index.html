EvoQuery Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Overview

EvoQuery is a Chrome extension that queries multiple AI models simultaneously and analyzes consensus across their responses. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and account management)
  • Usage counts (to enforce tier limits)

Query Data (Anonymized Research Data)

Important: When AI models significantly disagree on a response (consensus below 95%), we save anonymized query data for AI research purposes. This includes:

  • Your query text
  • AI model responses
  • Consensus metrics and divergence scores
  • Query mode (Quick or Deep)

This data is NOT linked to your account or email. It is stored separately and cannot be traced back to you.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Queries where AI models agree (95%+ consensus) are not stored
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Location data
  • Personal files or documents

How We Use Your Data

Account Data: Used solely for authentication, billing, and usage tracking.

Anonymized Query Data: May be used to:

  • Improve AI model accuracy and reliability
  • Create research benchmarks for AI disagreement patterns
  • License to AI research organizations for model improvement

How the Extension Works

When you use EvoQuery:

  • Your query is sent to our secure backend API
  • Our backend forwards the query to AI services (Claude, Grok, OpenAI, Google Gemini)
  • Responses are analyzed for consensus and returned to your browser
  • If models disagree significantly, anonymized data is saved for research
  • When AI models significantly disagree on a response (consensus below 95%), we save anonymized query data for AI research purposes.

Chrome Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API solely to save user interface preferences and authentication tokens. This data stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

EvoQuery uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude) - AI processing
  • xAI (Grok) - AI processing
  • OpenAI (GPT-4o Mini) - AI processing
  • Google (Gemini) - AI processing
  • Supabase - Database hosting
  • Railway - Backend hosting
  • LemonSqueezy - Payment processing

Please refer to each service's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Security

All communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS encryption. Our backend is hosted on Railway and our database on Supabase, both with industry-standard security practices.

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your account data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Contact us to exclude your queries from research data collection

Children's Privacy

EvoQuery is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
