EvoQuery Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2024

Overview

EvoQuery is a Chrome extension that queries multiple AI models simultaneously and analyzes consensus across their responses. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

How the Extension Works

When you use EvoQuery:

  • Your query is sent to our secure backend API
  • Our backend forwards the query to AI services (Claude, Grok, OpenAI)
  • Responses are returned to your browser and displayed
  • No queries or responses are stored on our servers
  • All processing is transient (temporary)

Chrome Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API solely to save user interface preferences. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services

EvoQuery uses the following third-party AI services to process queries:

  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • xAI (Grok)
  • OpenAI (GPT-4o Mini and embeddings)

Your queries are sent to these services through our backend API. Please refer to each service's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Security

All communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS encryption. Our backend is hosted on Railway with industry-standard security practices.

Children's Privacy

EvoQuery is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
